  
**Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist**

| Do's ✅ | Don'ts ❌ |
| ----- | ----- |
| **Audit your privacy settings regularly to control who can see your personal posts and tagged photos.** | **Don't post heat-of-the-moment rants or offensive content that could be flagged by future recruiters.** |
| **Highlight your achievements, such as certifications, hackathon wins, or club involvements (like GSoC).** | **Don't share sensitive personal info, like your home address, phone number, or current real-time location.** |
| **Network with professionals and alumni in your field (AI/ML, Dev) to build a "hidden" job market.** | **Don't engage with "rage-bait" or online arguments that could negatively impact your digital footprint.** |
| **Verify information before sharing news or infographics to avoid spreading misinformation.** | **Don't plagiarize content or claim someone else's project/code as your own without proper credit.** |
| **Use a professional profile picture and a clean bio on platforms like LinkedIn or Twitter/X.** | **Don't post photos of illegal activities or violations of university code, even on "private" stories.** |

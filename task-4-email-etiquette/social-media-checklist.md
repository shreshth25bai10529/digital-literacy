## ✅ Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

✔️ Do’s
Maintain a respectful and positive tone in your posts and comments.
Think before posting—ensure your content reflects your values and future goals.
Protect your privacy by using strong passwords and adjusting privacy settings.
Share useful and meaningful content that adds value to others.
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
❌ Don’ts
Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
Avoid oversharing personal information like location, phone number, etc.
Don’t engage in online arguments or cyberbullying.
Avoid posting when emotional or angry—it can harm your reputation.
Don’t trust or click on suspicious links or unknown sources.
